# Infrastructure

To operate the network, we obtain numerous infrastructure components.

## Software

Our infrastructure is heavily based on Linux. [NixOS](https://nixos.org/) is our
Operating System of choice. NixOS is managed by versioned configuration management.

## Hardware

We try to select Hardware based on energy and ressource efficiency. Our backend
infrastructure tries to optimize for power efficiency while our Access Point and
switching hardware is optimized on efficient use of natural ressources, thus usually
obtained from second-hand or recycling sources.
