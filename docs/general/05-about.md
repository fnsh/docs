# About

## Motivation

Freie Netze Südhessen e.V. was initially established from people affiliated with
Freifunk Darmstadt. The goal was to create a entity for the
[Freifunk Darmstadt](https://darmstadt.freifunk.net) project.

While Mesh-Networks are still operated by the association, the goal nowadays
is not limited at mere network operation.

## Goals

We want to create a environment in which ressources are available for individuals
to learn, apply and improve their skillset. One way or another, everything we did
is a result of this pragma.

The current accomplishments are a result of people with different interest and
skillsets coming together. In such an environment, we want to have a straight-forward
environment where these ressources are available to people who normally do not have
access to those ressources.

This is only possible with us enabling applied learning. Thus we try to not strive
for solutions which are 100% build to perfection. Individuals with passion require
a ledge to cling onto. Thus complexity shall be reduced in order to allow many to
understand a solution.

We try to assist in this process by documenting our assets and processes. In the
past, we've seen such intiatives die down due to insufficient documentation.
Documentation shall not be barricaded behind a login wall. While this is still
necessary in some regards (Passwords, Data privacy), we try to minimize this impact.

In addition we want to provide infrastructure to groups in the region of
southern-Hesse which are aligned to this ideal. They do not require to come
from a technical background.

## Get Involved

We try to do in-person meetings in Darmstadt, Hessen. This currently happens on
irregular basis. 

For communication we use Matrix. Our public room is [`#fnsh:matrix.tu-darmstadt.de`](https://matrix.to/#/%23fnsh:matrix.tu-darmstadt.de).

In case you whish to learn more about a specific area or want to be introduced to the group,
you are welcome to get in contact with someone experienced in this field. Just drop an E-Mail
to the person. A full list is available below at people.
